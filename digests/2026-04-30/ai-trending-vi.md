This marks a critical leap from "conversational assistance" to "environmental autonomy" for AI tools.

Unlike traditional agent frameworks emphasizing the "plan-execute" loop, newly emerging projects focus on the **modular encapsulation and cross-platform migration of capabilities**: mattpocock/skills directly open-sources personal `.claude` directories, ComposioHQ builds a Codex skill marketplace, and obra/superpowers proposes accompanying methodologies. This is directly related to the recent releases of products like OpenAI Codex and Claude Code; the community is building the "app store" infrastructure for coding agents.
